Welcome to the Quiz Time game! Here are the rules and guidelines for playing:

1. Starting the Game:
    To begin, type "YES" to proceed.

2. Gameplay:
    A series of questions will be displayed on the screen.
    Answer each question carefully.

3. Ending the Game:
    The game ends immediately if you answer any question incorrectly.

4. Scoring:
    Your total score will be displayed at the end of the game.

Enjoy the challenge and do your best to achieve a high score!
